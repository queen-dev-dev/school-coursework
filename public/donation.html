Donations

This is the space to donate to either the organisation or an individual artist.
(Note that some artists may take a donation without performing commission.)

Donations to the organisation are much appreciated, and help to keep the festival functioning. It helps fund refreshments during the event, as well as a communal fund for artist's gear which they may not be able to afford otherwise. Each donation can allow someone to follow their dreams. To donate, send money on PayPal to "Burnley CAF", "Donations@BCAF.co.uk", +44 7704 216802. Thank you :)
